About AttachéPro

Our Mission

AttachéPro is designed to streamline the industrial attachment process for students, assessors, and organizations.

Our platform provides a comprehensive solution for managing the entire industrial attachment lifecycle, from registration to final assessment.

Key Benefits

  • Centralized management of attachment records
  • Digital logbook submissions and assessments
  • Real-time communication between attachees and assessors
  • Video conferencing for remote assessments
  • Comprehensive reporting and analytics

Our Team

AttachéPro is developed by a team of dedicated professionals with experience in education, industry, and software development.

We are committed to continuously improving our platform based on user feedback and evolving industry needs.
